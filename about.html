Bio

I am a creative web application developer with a passion for user experience and interactive design, with two years of experience.

I reside in Parklands and I am a self-proclaimed rottweiler photographer. I work at IT Varsity, where my responsibilities include web development, tech support, and grading student work. My hobbies include hiking, playing video games, binging anime and Netflix like a true millennial, and obsessing over anything design.
